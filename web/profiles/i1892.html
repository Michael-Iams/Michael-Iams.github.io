    ObituaryTranscribed by Laura Nelle Poland Mallett on May 04, 2006. [72]
    Benjamin Iams, a well known resident of Center township, died at his home,
    near Hopewell, Saturday morning, November 15 age 74 years. Mr. Iams was a son
    of Dennis and Matilda Huffman Iams, deceased, and had spent practically his
    entire in the neighborhood, in which he died. He was a member of the
    Hopewell M. E. church and was highly respected. Besides his wife he is survived by
    nine children, as follows: William Iams, of Washington; Daniel, of Sycamore;
    Charles, of Amity; Elzie of Waynesburg; Mrs. Martha Babbitt, of New York;
    Mrs. Priscilla King, of Amity; Miss Cora, Harry and Dennis Iams, at home. The
    funeral services were held on Monday afternoon. Interment in the Hopewell
    cemetery.
    "Note" Hand written on my obit is the date Nov. 29, 1913. Also Waynesburg Republican, Waynesburg, Greene Co., Pa.

E. church and was highly respected. Besides his wife he is survived by nine children, as follows: William Iams, of Washington; Daniel, of Sycamore; Charles, of Amity; Elzie of Waynesburg; Mrs. Martha Babbitt, of New York; Mrs. Priscilla King, of Amity; Miss Cora, Harry and Dennis Iams, at home. The funeral services were held on Monday afternoon. Interment was in the Hopewell Cemetery.
