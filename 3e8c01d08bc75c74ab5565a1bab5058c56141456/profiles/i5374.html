ObituaryMar 29, 1940The Dailey Examiner, Bellefontaine, Logan, Ohio, United States
MARGUERITE "KNOX" IAMS

Passes Away in Her Home Here

Funeral of Mrs Perry Iams Will be Held Sunday.

Mrs. Perry Iams, died at 6:30 p.m. Thursday at the family home, 812 South Main Street.

She entered White Cross Hospital, in Columbus, for observation Monday, but she remained there only until Wednesday, her condition becoming alarming.

She died of uremic poisoning and complications.

Remained with her husband are the following brothers and sisters; Frank Knox, of West Mansfield; Ben Knox and Mrs. Clara Bailey, of Bellefontaine; Lennie Knox, Mrs. Cora Simon and Mrs. Bertha Lansdown, of Raymond, Mrs. Monalete Hoy, of Marysville; Harold Knox, of Columbus, and Richard Knox, of Waterville.

The mother, Mrs. Lunetta Knox, of Raymond, also remains.

The deceased was the daughter of Alonzo and Lunetta Orahood Knox. She was born in Raymond, May 31, 1905.

She united in marriage with Perry Iams in April of 1932.

Funeral services will be held Sunday at 1:30 p.m. at the family residence, 812 South Main Street, with Rev. John S. Forsythe, of Raymond, in charge.

Burial will be in the Raymond Cemetery.

Published in The Dailey Examiner, Bellefontaine, Ohio, March 29, 1940

In Memory of my G-Aunt Marquerite
