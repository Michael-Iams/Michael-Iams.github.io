  • Deed of Gift
    Page: 271
    Grantor: John Duvalle, AA Co., planter
    Grantee: Hester Ijams, AA Co., spinster
    Date of Deed: 16 April 1705
    Date Recorded: none
    Consideration: Given as a life estate, with the condition that she not
    marry or have other children. At her death, her children, Ann and
    Elizabeth, were to inherit when they had reached the age of 16 or
    married. If all should die, then the land and chattels would go to
    Benjamin Burges.
    Signature: John Duvalle
    Witnesses: Samuel Chambers, Charles Hammond
    Description: The uppermost part of a tract called Burgesses choice,
    bounded by a parcel sold out of the land to Richard Ijams, by
    Lewis Duvalle's land, by a parcel sold out of the original tract to
    Joseph Burton. Also a" ... Schedule hereunto annexed ... "
    Other Pertinent Information: An inventory of goods and chattels follows,
    which includes three servants: Richard Cloke, James Brotherton,
    and J osias Harrison.
    Release of Dower: Elizabeth Duvalle
