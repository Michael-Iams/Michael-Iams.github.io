Kathryn Imes - Mead: Newspaper Obituary and Death Notice
Idaho Spokesman-Review (Coeur d'Alene, ID) - Tuesday, June 9, 1998
Deceased Name: Kathryn Imes - Mead
Funeral for Kathryn Marie Imes, 37, is set for 1 p.m. Thursday at the Mukogawa Fort Wright Chapel. Burial service will follow at Riverside Cemetery. Riplinger Funeral Home is in charge of arrangements.

Mrs. Imes, who was born in Bellingham, died Wednesday of cancer.

She graduated in 1978 from Liberty Bell High School in Winthrop, Wash., and moved to Spokane in 1981.

In 1984 she married Randy Imes.

Mrs. Imes was a homemaker.

Survivors include her husband; two daughters, Ashley and Whitney Imes, and a son, Adam Imes, all at home; her mother and stepfather, Karen and Charles Flagg of Twisp, Wash.; her father, John Tanguy of Mill Creek, Wash.; three brothers, Scot and Craig Tanguy, both of Spokane, and Steve Tanguy of Palm Springs, Calif.; and her grandparents, Mr. and Mrs. Walter Tanguy of Bellingham.
