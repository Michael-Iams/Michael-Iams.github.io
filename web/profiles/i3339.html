    FRANCIS A. IAMS: Newspaper Obituary and Death Notice

    Orlando Sentinel, The (FL) - Tuesday, September 26, 1995
    Deceased Name: FRANCIS A. IAMS
    92, Kentia Road, Casselberry, died Saturday, Sept. 23. Mr. Iams was a sales representative for recreational vehicle company. Born in Pittsburgh, he moved to Central Florida in 1952. Survivors: wife, Dorothy; daughters, Nancy Heriff, Casselberry, Jo Ann Abt, Longwood; three grandchildren; two great-grandchildren. Baldwin-Fairchild Funeral Home, Altamonte Springs.
