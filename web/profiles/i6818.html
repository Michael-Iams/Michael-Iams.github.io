    Obituary
    Charles L. Imes, 73, of Hemet, Calif., passed from us Monday, Oct. 26, 2009, very peacefully at home with his wife and sons by his side.

    He was born June 6, 1936, in Preble County, Ohio, to Homer S. Imes and Virginia M. (Izor) Imes. Charles was a 30-year aerospace engineer at Rhor Industries in Riverside, Calif. He retired from there in 1995. He was active in Little League and Scouting as the children grew. He spent many hours working in his church as he was needed. He became active in politics in his little community.

    He spent the major part of his retirement working in the music industry with his wife Shirley, traveling all the Southern California area. They retired from the traveling in 2005 but still would be invited to sing in various places.

    He is survived by his wife of 54 years, Shirley (Dickson) Imes; two sons, James (Kathy) Imes of Phoenix, Ariz., and Michael (Danielle) Imes of Riverside, Calif.; his brother, Frank (Marcia) Imes of Phoenix, Ariz.; eight grandchildren; 12 great-grandchildren; and many nieces and nephews.

    He was preceded in death by his parents; and youngest brother, Samuel Imes.

    Charles spent many happy years growing up in Ohio and moved to San Diego, Calif., in 1955. Work brought the family north, finally settling in the quiet little retirement community of Hemet, Calif.

    He was a loving husband and devoted father and grandfather. His personality and kindness to others endeared him to all who knew him, and he will be greatly missed.

    Private services for family only were held Tuesday, Oct. 27, at the Imes home. He will be cremated and his ashes scattered at sea.
