Lyman Huber IamsObituarySep 11, 1989Akron, Summit, Ohio, United States
Lyman H. Iams: Newspaper Obituary and Death Notice

Akron Beacon Journal (OH) - Monday, September 11, 1989
Deceased Name: Lyman H. Iams
Lyman H. Iams, 76, of Green Township, died Sept. 10 after a long illness. Born in Pennsylvania, he had lived in the Akron area most of his life. He retired from Goodyear Tire & Rubber Co. after 28 years service. He served in the U. S. Army in World War II, and had attended the Greensburg United Methodist Church.

He is survived by wife, Kathryn; daughter and son-in-law, Helen and David Clingerman of Denver, Colo.; stepdaughter, Janet Wagner of Fairlawn; stepson, John Draper of Minerva; brother, Victor Iams of West Virginia; sisters, Sarah Wood and Bernice Wilson, both of West Virginia, Rosemary Rausch of Plain City; five grandchildren; and two great-grandchildren.

No calling hours. Memorial service will be held Thursday, 2 p.m., at Greensburg U.M. Church, 2161 Greensburg Rd., with Rev. Donald Newland officiating. (Schermesser, Suburban South, 724-9351.)

Kathryn Irene ShowObituaryMar 20, 1993Akron Beacon Journal, Akron, Summit, Ohio, United States
Kathryn I. Iams (Draper), 84, a former resident of Green Township for over 50 years, died March 19, 1993 at Akron City Hospital.

Born in Confluence, Pa., she was an area homemaker most of her life. She was a member of the Greensburg United Methodist Church.

She is survived by son, John (Theresa) Draper of Alliance; daughter, Janet (Jackson) Wagner of Fairlawn; brother, Carl Show of Pennsylvania; sisters Betty Spurgeon of Florida and Freda Crumrine of Pennsylvania; grandchildren Ross Wagner of Waukegan, Ill., Jennifer Wagner of the home, Beth Draper and Dan Draper, both of Alliance.

Funeral service Monday, 11 a.m., at the Schermesser Funeral Home, Suburban South Chapel, 600 E. Turkeyfoot Lake Rd. (Route 619), Rev. Ross Wagner officiating. Interment at Greensburg Cemetery. Calling hours Sunday, 3 to 5p.m., and Monday, 10:15 to 11 a.m. (Schermesser, Suburban South Chapel
