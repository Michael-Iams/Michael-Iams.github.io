    IAMES, George D.-----Son of Mr and Mrs W. O. Iames, 405 Dewy Ave. Born 22 February 1882. Was commissioned 1st Lieutenant in the Engineer Corps08 August 1918. He was assigned to Camp Humphreys, and was honorably dischargedfrom the service of the US 15 Oct 1918.
