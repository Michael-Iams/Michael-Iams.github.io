Obituary
Lake of the Ozarks, Mo.

Johnie M. Imes, 88, of Columbia, Mo., formerly of Evansville, Ind., passed away Tuesday, Jan. 4, 2011, at St. Mary's Medical Center.

Johnie was born Aug. 9, 1922, in Stephenville, Texas, the daughter of Lonnie and Annie (Carter) McBride. She graduated from the School of Nursing at Methodist Hospital in St. Joseph, Mo., and worked as a registered nurse in Evansville during WWII. Johnie also worked as a registered nurse in Missouri as well as being a Professor at Northwest Missouri State University teaching accounting and finance.

Johnie was an avid golfer. She was Rules Chairman for Missouri Women's Golf and the winner of the Bernice Edlen Award, given to her by the Missouri Woman's Golf Association. Johnie was an avid supporter of the University of Missouri Woman's Golf Team and had an intercollegiate Woman's Golf Tournament named for her, The Johnie Imes, played at Old Hawthorne Country Club in Columbia, Mo. She also enjoyed playing duplicate bridge and was a Bronze Life Master. Johnie was an ardent fisherwoman all of her life. She fished with her husband at Lake of the Ozarks, Bull Shoals Lake and in Canada and the Arctic Circle, with daughter Pam, in Arkansas and Missouri. Johnie will always be remembered for her sunny disposition and for her love of life, friends and especially her family.

She is survived by daughter, Pam Adkison (Doug) of Evansville, granddaughter, Kerri Klein Fletcher (Themba), great grandson, Emmett Eugene Fletcher, granddaughter, Annie MacDonald, great grandchildren, Daniel, Elizabeth and Alexa, grandson, Brian Imes and grandson, Bradley Imes (Michele).

She was preceded in death by her beloved husband of 56 years, Elvin D. Imes and a daughter, Carolyn E. Imes.

Funeral Services will be held at 10 am, Saturday, Jan. 8, 2011, at Missouri United Methodist Church, 204 S. Ninth St., Columbia, Missouri with Reverend Marla Weller officiating. Burial will be in Memorial Park Cemetery in Columbia, Missouri.

Friends may call from 5-8 p.m., Friday, Jan. 7, 2011, at Memorial Funeral Home, 1217 Business Loop 70 West, Columbia, Mo.
Boone Funeral Home - East Chapel, 5330 Washington Avenue, Evansville will be handling local arrangements.
