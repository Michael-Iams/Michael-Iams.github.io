IOWA STATE NEWS

- Ruthven is said to have the oldest married couple in the state in Mr. and
Mrs. John Iams. They were married sixty-seven years ago in Pennsylvania.
...

Long Wedded Iowan. 

Washington, Ia., Dec. 24 - Mr. and Mrs. John Iams of Washington county are the longest married of any couple in the state. If they survive until next September they will have been wedded seventy-two years. Mr. Iams was born in Greene county, Pennsylvania, April 13, 1809, and his wife, who is a native of Washington county in the same state, is only two months his junior. Soon after their marriage they removed to Licking and then to Newark, Ohio, but they left that state in 1838 to come to Iowa. They have since resided here. 

THE CEDAR RAPIDS EVENING GAZETTE, Cedar Rapids, Iowa, Saturday, December 24, 1898.
