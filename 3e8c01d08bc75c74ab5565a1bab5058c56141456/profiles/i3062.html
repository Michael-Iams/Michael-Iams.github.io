    ObituaryApr 16, 1967Lima News, Lima, Allen, Ohio, United States
    James (Sam) Iiames, 90, of 628 N. West, died at 9:50 p.m. Friday in Memorial Hospital.

    Born Feb. 9, 1877 in Shelby County, he was a son of Samuel and Mary Ann Abbott Iiames. He was married to Myrtle Iiames, who died Feb. 12, 1966.

    Mr. Iiames was a member of St. Mark's Methodist Church and a retired employe of Superior Coach Corp.

    Surviving are two sons, Howard T. of 516 N. Elizabeth and Raymond M. of 1118 Harrison; one daughter, Mrs. Donald E. (Ruth) Brown Sr. of 628 N. West; one brother, Frank of 340 W. O'Connor; eight grandchildren and eight great-grandchildren.

    Services will be 10:30 a.m. Tuesday in Chiles and Sons-Laman Chapel with Rev. Frank Rowe officiating. Burial will be in Memorial Park Cemetery.

    Friends may call after noon today at the funeral home.

    (published in The Lima News, Sunday, April 16, 1967)
