    1979

    Services Held For Boyd Iiams

    Boyd Iiams of rural Murray died Sunday, Sept. 14, at Clarke County Hospital in Osceola at the age of 77. He had lived in Clarke County most of his life.

    Born on July 2, 1903, in Madison County, he was the son of Arthur and Florence Kirk Iiams. He was united in marriage to Daisy Ruth Woosley on May 9, 1925, at Chariton. She preceded him in death on Sept. 12, 1974. A farmer, Mr. Iiams had lived in Clarke county most of his life.

    Survivors include three children, Darlene I. Morris of Des Moines, Emma Creveling of Osceola and Dwayne of Murray; six grandchildren; five great-grandchildren; two brothers, Dale of Murray and Nathan of Mollolla, Ore.; and two sisters, Velma Gibbons of Kress, Texas and Thelma Thurman of Kearney, Neb. He was also preceded in death by an infant child, his parents, two brothers and two sisters.

    Funeral services were held at 1:30 p.m. Wednesday, Sept. 17 at the Murray Church of Christ with burial at the Union Cemetery near Murray. Watts-Lindsay Funeral Home in Murray was in charge of arrangements.
