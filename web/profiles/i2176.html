ObituaryTranscribed by Laura Nelle Poland Mallett on March 08, 2006 [72]
Kate Orndoff Iams, 81, of 252 South Cumberland Street, Waynesburg, died at 6 PM, Monday, Oct. 3, 1977, in the Greene County Memorial Hospital after a long illness.

Born Aug. 22, 1896 in Center Township, Greene County, she was the daughter of the late William H. And Martha Ullom Orndoff.
She was the widow of Glenn G. Iams who died Friday, Sept. 30, 1977.
She was a school teacher and also cooked for several restaurants for several years. She had spent all of her life in Waynesburg and Center Township.
She attended the Hopewell United Methodist Church and was a member of the daughters of America.
Surviving are a daughter, Mrs. William "Martha" King of Larimer; one son, Robert S. Iams of Rancho, California; three grandchildren and two great-grandchildren.
One son, Frederic, three sisters and three brothers are deceased.
