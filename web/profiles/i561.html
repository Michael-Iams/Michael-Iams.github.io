Casper Star Tribune
April 16, 2004

Yvonne N. 'Aunt Bonnie' Iams
WHEATLAND - Memorial services for former Glendo resident Yvonne N. "Aunt Bonnie" Iams, 78, will be conducted at 10 a.m. today, April 14, at Platte County Memorial Nursing Home in Wheatland by Pastor Clyde McGee. Interment will follow inHorseshoe Cemetery in Glendo.??Afterward, a reception for family and friends will be held at Glendo Community Church.??She died April 10, 2004, at Platte County Memorial Hospital in Wheatland.??Born Nov. 30, 1925, in Rotan, Texas, she was thedaughter of Jack and Jimmie (Boulton) Newman; was raised and educated in Lefors, Texas; and was a member of the First Baptist Church there.
In 1946, she married Harvey Owen Loyd in Wickett, Texas. He preceded her in death on May 14, 1975, after 32 years of marriage.??She worked at a clothing factory in McLean, Texas, and was a telephone operator while in Texas; and then was apostmistress in California. In 1970, she moved to Glendo and was employed as a cook.??On May 26, 1978, she married Herbert E. Iams in Casper. He died May 4, 1997.??At the time of her death, she was a resident of the nursing home.??She enjoyedplaying Yahtzee, reading, and fishing.??Survivors include three children, Douglas R. Loyd of Oklahoma, Linda C. Joseph of Allentown, Pa., and Pam Dipdahl of Arizona; three siblings and their spouses, Jackie D. Newman of Phoenix, Sharon Kay McGeeof Norman, Okla., and Larry Ray Newman of Ketchikan, Alaska; three half-sisters; a half-brother; 10 grandchildren; 12 great-grandchildren; and several beloved nieces and nephews.??In addition to her husbands, she was preceded in death by herparents.??The family would appreciate memorials to Platte County Memorial Nursing Home, P.O. Box 848, Wheatland, WY 82201.??Gorman Funeral Homes-Platte Chapel of Wheatland is in charge of arrangements.
