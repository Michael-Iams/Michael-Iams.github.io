Minnie Lee Myers Ijames b. April 1, 1880 d. Dec. 18, 1955
"MRS. B. G. IJAMES
HIGH POINT, Dec. 18 - Mrs. Minnie MYERS IJAMES, 75 - of 2500 English street, widow of Berry G. IJAMES, died in High Point Memorial Hospital this morning. (paragraph) Although she had been in declining health her death was unexpected. (paragraph) She was born in Davidson County, daughter of David R. and Eliza Elizabeth HINKLE MYERS and had lived in the High Point community since 1903. Mrs. IJAMES was a member of the Peoples Methodist Church and the adult Sunday School Class. Her husband died in 1949. (paragraph) Surviving are three daughters, Mrs. Evia HIGH of the home, Mrs. John FERREE of Seagrove and Mrs. C. M. WOOD of High Point; three sons, Athel M. and Protus F. Ijames of High Point and Bruce C. IJAMES of Washington, D.C.; one sister, Mrs. L. T. Anderson of Lexington; three brothers, June, Vance, and Grover Myers, all of Lexington; 11 grandchildren and one great-grandchild. (paragraph) Funeral services will be conducted at the Peoples Methodist Church at 2 p.m. Saturday. The body will be placed in the church at 1:30 p.m. Burial will be in the Pleasant Grove methodist Church Cemetary. "
Note: This obituary contains two errors. "Mrs. C.M. Wood" is actually "Mrs. C. W.(Curtis William) Wood" and "Bruce C. Ijames" was actually Bruce G. (Glenn) Ijames".
