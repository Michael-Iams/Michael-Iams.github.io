    Elmora Ijames, son Dovie G. Ijames and the late Robert Elmore Ijames who died on January 25, 2004, was born May 14,1959 in Rowan County, North Carolina. He departed this life Sunday, April 14, 2019 at his sisters’ home located at 266 Joe Summers Road in Woodleaf, North Carolina.
    He attended West Rowan High School and was formerly employed with Apple Baking Company. He was also self-employed car mechanic. He was a visiting member of Chestnut Grove Baptist Church. In addition to his father, he was also preceded in death by his wife, Darlene J. Ijames in May of 2006 and a brother, Robert Lee Ijames in March of 2004.

    Those who will cherish his memory, his mother Dovie G. Ijames of Cleveland, NC; his daughter, Sherry Oglesby of Salisbury, NC; two sisters, Sally I. Dulin of Woodleaf NC and LaVerne I. Barber of Cleveland, NC; four grandchildren, Hydeya, Tempest, Takiya, and Elijah Robbins, all of Salisbury, NC; nephews, cousins, and a host of other family and friends.

    Celebration of Life services will be conducted on Friday, April 19, 2019, 1:00 p.m. at Chestnut Grove Church. Members of the family will receive friends at the church from 12-1:00p.m. but will assemble at the residence of 266 Joe Summers Road, Woodleaf, N.C., at all other times. Visit the book of memories for Mr. Elmora Ijames at www.rutledgeandbigham.com. Rutledge and Bigham is serving the Ijames family.
