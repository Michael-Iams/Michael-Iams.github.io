    ObituaryMay 26, 1917The Dalles, Wasco, Oregon, United States [2144]
    The Dalles, Or., May 26

    Mrs. Mary Elizabeth Wickman, who died recently at her home near The Dalles, was one of the earliest of Oregon's pioneers. She crossed the plains when a girl of 13 years and resided in Oregon continuously until her death. She was married to Frederic Wickman, July 1, 1868, and eight[sic] children were born to them, Elizabeth, Frederic, William, Henry, Ella, Francis and Leo. The latter died in infancy. Mr. Wickman died a number of years ago.

    Besides here children, Mrs. Wickman leaves two brothers, William Schuster of Goldendale, Wash. and Charles Schuster of Seattle and two sisters, Mrs. Eliza Schanno of The Dalles and Mrs. Rose Maskelyn of Walla Walla.
