    Dennis Iams, a prominent citizen of Richhill Township and a life long resident of Greene County, died at his home near Wind Ridge, Friday afternoon, Aug. 22, aged 72 years, following a year's illness due to infirmities of age. Mr. Iams was a son of William and Hannah Wright Iams, deceased. He was a successful farmer and stock raiser. Beside his wife, Mrs. Emma Iams, he leaves the following children: Levi W. Iams, of Ruffdale; William Iams, of West Finley; James Iams, of Detroit, Michigan Mrs. Richard Goslin, of Waynesburg, and Mrs. Martha L. Scott, of Amity. The funeral services were held Sunday at 2 o'clock, conducted by the Rev. A. M. Riggs, pastor of the Wind Ridge Presbyterian Church. Interment in the Braddock Cemetery.
