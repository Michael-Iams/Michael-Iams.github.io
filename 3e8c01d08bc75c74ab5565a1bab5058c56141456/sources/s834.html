Page: p 156

  • Page: 306
    Grantor: Richard Jiams, Sen., AA Co., planter, and his wife
    Grantee: Richard Snowden, AA Co., Gent
    Date of Deed: 8 June 1727
    Date Recorded: September 27th 1727
    Consideration: £45.11.00
    Signature: Richd Jiams, Sen.
    Witnesses: John Welsh, Moses Maccubbin, William Fish
    Description: That parcel containing 50 acres, bought by Jiams from
    Benjamin Burges 1 October 1700, called Jiams Purchase, being part of
    parcel called Burges's Choice. Also all that parcel containing 61 acres,
    bought by Jiams 7 February 1704, from Benjamin Burgess called Jiams
    last purchase, being part of tract called Burges's Choice. With the
    exception of the 25 acres by a spring, sold to John Jacobs.
    Other Pertinent Information: It is the true intent of this deed that if Jiams
    repay the consideration money with interest by 10 October 1728, this
    deed will be void.
