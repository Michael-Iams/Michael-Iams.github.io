    In his Will, Thomas Wooden (Wills 1833 Liber. 14, Folio 331-332) Hall of
    Records, Annapolis, Md., then a widower leaves one acre of land on his
    plantation for a Family Graveyard. He left it to his heirs and assigns forever.

    The graveyard is situated in the City of Baltimore, Md. at the south corners of
    Rosedale & Presstman Sts.

    The graveyard was sold May 27, 1918 to one F. Henry Maisch, sale made by
    Mr. Leigh Bonsall.
