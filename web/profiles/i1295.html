    Elizabeth Iams, mother of County Supt. Iams, died at Summit Hill, Ross Co., Ohio, July 22, 1891. The deceased was a sister of the late Hon. Thomas Iams; was born and raised at Iams' station and had been a resident of our county until within the last three years. We have the highest authority for saying that she was a noble mother, and for more than forty years a consistent member of the Methodist Episcopal Church, and in the triumph of her faith she died, and has gone to join the company of the Christian dead; and other friends besides the little family of Supt. Iams is called to mourn her loss.
    "Note written on my obit is the date Aug. 6, 1891...LNPM
