    ObituaryTranscribed by Laura Nelle Poland Mallett on May 05, 2006 [72]
    Sad and Fatal Accident
    A son of Thomas Iams, Associate Judge elect, of Morris Township, aged about fifteen years, met with an accident on Saturday last which terminated in death in less than an hour after it's occurrence. The young man was hauling lumber with two horses and a wagon, in attempting to get upon the wagon his foot slipped and he fell to the ground, the wheel or wheels passing over his bowels with the above result. He only spoke twice, we learned, after the accident. The parents, brothers and sisters of the deceased have our heartfelt sympathies in their sad and sudden bereavement.
    "Note" hand written on my obit is the date Nov. 8, 1871..LNPM
