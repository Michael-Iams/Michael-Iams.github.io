ObituaryFeb 22, 1966Lima, Allen, Ohio, United States
Mrs. Myrtle Mae Iiames, 79, of 628 N. West, died at 6:45 p.m. Monday in Memorial Hospital where she had been a patient three days.

Born Jan. 11, 1887, in Allen County, she was the daughter of Theodore and Mary Stoner Wood. She married James (Sam) Iiames April 14, 1906, and he survives.

Mrs. Iiames was a member of St. Mark's Methodist Church and the Women's Benefit Association.

Also surviving are two sons, Howard, 516 N. Elizabeth, and Raymond, 1118 Harrison; a daughter, Mrs. Donald (Ruth) Brown, Beaverdam; three sisters, Mrs. Minnie Cahill, Cairo, Mrs. J. J. (Plezia) Miller, 617 N. Collett, and Mrs. Laura Flanigan, 628 1/2 N. West; eight grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren.

Services will be at 1 p.m. Thursday in Chiles and Sons-Laman Funeral Home, Rev. Frank Rowe officiating, with burial in Memorial Park Cemetery. Friends may call at the funeral home after 7 p.m. today.

(published in The Lima News, Tuesday, February 22, 1966)
