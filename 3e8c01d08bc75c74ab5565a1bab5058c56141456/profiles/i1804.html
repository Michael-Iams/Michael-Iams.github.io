    Was an active participant in the Whiskey Rebellion (insurrectionist), and after the rebellion
    was the first of 11 to take the oath of allegience to the Constitution.
    (Iams, EB. History of John Iiams, and Elizabeth Hampton Clans)

    Served in Colonel John Stone's Regiment in the Revolutionary War
    (enlisting 2 Jun 1779, discharged 1 Nov 1780; Maryland Archives Vol XVIII pp. 126)

    Removed to the Ten Mile Area, PA; in about 1784
    (Leckey, p 481)
