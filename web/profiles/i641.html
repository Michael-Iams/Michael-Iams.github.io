    Obituary
    Charlotte Donnan Iams - Received award for leadership, community service, 84, of North Watson Ave., Washington, died Thursday, June 15, 1995, in Humbert Lane Health Care Center, following a lengthy illness.

    She was born Aug. 12, 1910, in Pittsburgh, a daughter of Dr. Jay Donald and Grace Donnan Iams.

    Miss Iams was a graduate of Pennsylvania College for Women, now Chatham College, Pittsburgh. She was a teacher.
    She was a longtime member of Washington County League of Women Voters, and was honored in 1977 with the Anna Estes Strawbridge Honor Roll Award for outstanding leadership and services to the community.

    She was the last of her immediate family. Surviving are two nephews, Jay D. Iams of Columbus, Ohio, and William M. Iams of St. Paul, Minn.; two nieces, Mary Iams Daniel of Brownsville, Texas, and Julie Iams Regan of Richmond, Va., and several great-nieces and great-nephews. Deceased are three brothers, Alvan Donnan Iams, Alexander Murdock Iams and Frank Pierce Iams.

    A graveside service will be held at a later date at Allegheny County Cemetery, Pittsburgh. In lieu of flowers, the family suggests memorial contributions to Washington County League of Women Voters, in care of Judy Hughes, 112 Inverness Drive, McMurray, Pa. 15317. Arrangements are being handled Piatt and Barnhill Funeral Directors Inc
