    Capt. Thomas H. Ijams - was born 1841 in Ohio, Fairfield County, enlisted Sept. 5, 1861, mustered out September 23, 1865 as a Captain. Served with Co. K, 9th Reg. Indiana Infantry. He was a member of G.A.R. Post #14. In 1879 he moved to Chicago, Illinois
