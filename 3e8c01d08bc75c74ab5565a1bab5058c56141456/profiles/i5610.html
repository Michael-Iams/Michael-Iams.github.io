1.DANIEL1 WRIGHTSMAN,"RIGHTSMAN" was born Abt. 1826 in PA, and died Abt. 1897 in Mann Twp,Bedford Co. PA.He married (1) NANCY SHIPLEY 1851 in Green Ridge, Mann Twp,Bedford Co;PA, daughter of LEVIN SHIPLEY and HONORBARNES.She was born September 30, 1819 in S'Hampton-Mann Twp,PA, and died January 27, 1871 in S'Hampton-Mann Twp,PA.He married (2) SARAH B. CLINE, IAMES Bet. 1873 - 1875 in Mann Twp,Bedford Co. PA.She was born November 17, 1824 in PA, and died December 28, 1896 in Mann Twp,Bedford Co. PA. 
Notes for DANIEL WRIGHTSMAN,"RIGHTSMAN":
At this time it is not known for sure where Daniel was born. I could only find him on the PA census for 1860-1880. I am now looking at 1850 census for all states. 1870 census was taken at Southampton Twp, Bedford Co, PA. I knowthat Daniel remarried after Nancy's death in 1871, to a Sarah B. Iames, who was a widow with childrenfrom 2 previous marriages. More about her under Notes of Sarah B. Rightsman..I have deeds showing that Daniel and Sarah sold 123 acres to his son Elijah and wife Mary C. in 1885. This was the farm Daniel and Nancy lived on.I also have other deeds of property sold by them. I have directory listings of him and Sarah for 1878-79 of Mann Twp.1886 does not show Daniel, only Sarah and Elijah are shown in Mann Twp. Daniel was no longer a land owner, since he had sold his land to Elijah.I believe that Daniel is buried at Fairview Cemetery with Nancy without a marker. Helen Jay, that keeps all those records told me there are people buried there, but they don't know who. No record was kept that early,only later with those that had markers. I feel sure that Elijah's children, David and Chester are the otherbodies buried next to Nancy. PA did not require deaths and births to be registered until after 1900. Since the last year that taxes were paid for Daniel was in 1897. I strongly feel that he died that year. I have found nothing on Daniel since then. In 1833, Bedford CO, PA a Mrs. Mary Wrightsman married a widower William England, since I have never found another family with the Wrightsman name in Bedford Co, PA I feel that she was Daniel's mother, a widow who's husband must have died young with a small child. I have had no luck in finding the name of her deceased husband or where she moved with her new husband as they sold their property in PA and no more records could I find of them. 
Update: Records from Bedford Court House show that a Daniel Wrightsman was placed in the Poor House of Snake Township, Bedford County PA. 1833. The proprietor and his wife bonded Daniel until he was 16 years old. Children were only placed there if they were orphan, born out of wedlock or abandoned. I am sure this is our Daniel. Perhaps his mother had died shortly after her marriage to William England. Another brick wall that needs solving. 
Notes for NANCY SHIPLEY:
In the Imler Cemetery records, Vol. 3 of Fairview Church, Green Ridge, Mann Twp, PA;lists a Nancy, wife of D. Rightsman, b.Sept 30,1819d.Jan 27,1871, also Honor, daughter of D and N Rightsman, d. Aug 17,1872, 16yr,6mo,7days. I have a picture of her tombstone.Nancy was from the Shipley's of the MD family, that moved to PA area in 1802. Her father was Levin Shipley.The 1860 Pa Census lists Nancy wife of Daniel.The 1870 PA census, of the same township of Southampton, Mann, PA,lists wife of Daniel as Mary Ann, there is no doubt that they are the same person. In 1868 Mary Ann Rightsman and Daniel Rightsman joined the Church of Christ Union Meeting House, Green Ridge, Bedford Co. PA later know as the Fairview United Church of Christ, Artemas PA. This could have been the daughter Mary Jane. 
Notes for SARAH B. CLINE, IAMES:
Sarah was twice widowed. Her will states a son John M Kline, a daughter Eliza M. Morse, and a son James S. Iames. She also lists a daughter Mary J. Penrod,a son,James Iames.In an1878-79 Index listing for Mann Twp of Bedford Co. Pa lists a Daniel Rightman, Sarah Rightman and Eliza Rightman. Index is at the Pioneer Genealogy Society of Bedford, PA. page 88. I have a copy of this page. This was our Elijah. The spelling was wrong.The 1886 index shows Elijah Rightsman,and Sarah B. Rightsman living in Mann Twp.
