Copyright New York Times Company Sep 30, 1990


Virginia Clay Corpening and Henry Wigglesworth Ijams were married yesterday at St. Mary's Episcopal Church in High Point, N.C. The Rev. Glenn Busch and the Rev. Marvin C. Perry, a great-uncle of the bride, officiated. The bride is a daughter of Virginia T. Corpening and A. Glenn Corpening 3d, both of High Point. Her husband is a son of Mr. and Mrs. Seton Ijams of Bowdoinham, Me.
Mrs. Ijams, 26 years old, graduated from Salem College in Winston-Salem, N.C. She is a commercial banking lender at the Bank of New York in Manhattan. Mr. Ijams, 29, graduated from the Rochester Institute of Technology. He is an assistant vice president of Citibank in Tarrytown, N.Y.
