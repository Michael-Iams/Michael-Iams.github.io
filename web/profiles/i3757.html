    Lima News obit.

    Howard T. Iiames, 89, died at 9:56 p.m. Oct. 29, 1996, at St.Rita's Medical Center.
    He was born Oct. 11, 1907, in Lima to James A and Myrtle Wood Iiames. On Dec. 23, 1928, he married Edith Dempster, who survives.

    Mr. Iiames was a plumber with the Gast Plumbing and Heating Co., for 50 years. He was a member of St. Mark United Methodist Church. He played baseball for the Lima All Stars baseball team during the 1920s and 1930s. He was a member of the Knights of Pythias, Lima.

    Survivors also include a daughter, Norma Jean (Bud) Kihm of Lima; a sister, Ruth Mae Born of Naples, Fla.,; and a grandson.He was preceded in death by a brother, Raymond Iiames.

    Services will begin at 10 a.m. Saturday at the West Wayne Street Chapel of Chiles and Sons-Laman Funeral Home.The Rev. Howard Allison will officiate.
    Burial will be in Memorial Park Cemetery. Friends may call from 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 p.m. Friday at the funeral home.

    Memorial contributions may be made to the American Heart Association.
