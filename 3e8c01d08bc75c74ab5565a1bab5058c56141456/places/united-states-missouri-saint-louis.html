Barbara J. Ijames: Newspaper Obituary and Death Notice
St. Louis Post-Dispatch (MO) - Tuesday, November 1, 1994
Deceased Name: Barbara J. Ijames
Funeral services for Barbara J. "Bobbie" Ijames, 59, were held Monday at Baue Funeral Home, 311 Wood Street, O'Fallon. Burial was at St. Charles Memorial Gardens.

Mrs. Ijames, a former resident of O'Fallon, died Friday (Oct. 28, 1994) in Danville, Mo. She was a homemaker.

Among survivors are her husband, Bernie D. Ijames of New Florence, Mo.; five sons, Eddie Dean Barton, Preston D. Ijames, Vernon D. Ijames and Nolan D. Ijames, all of St. Paul, and Jason D. Ijames of O'Fallon; a daughter, Judy K. Hefti of St. Charles; and two sisters, Jerry Brock of O'Fallon and Carol LaFave of Mount Vernon, Mo.; one grandchild and one great-grandchild.

Memorials can be made to the donor's choice in care of Baue Funeral Home, 311 Wood Street, O'Fallon, 63366.
