March 7, 1973
STATE OF NEW MEXICO COUNTY OF TAOS IN THE PROBATE COURT IN THE MATTER OF THE ESTATE OF JESSE DORSEY IAMS. DECEASED Probate No 2U2 NOTICE OF HEARING FOR PROVING LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT OF JESSE DORSEY IAMS, Deceased.

TO WHOM IT MAY CONCERN Notice is hereby given that on the 31st day of January. 1973, an instrument purported to be the Last Will and Testament ot Jesse Dorsey lams, deceased, was offered for probate in the Probate Court of Taos County, New Mexico. Any and all persons objecting to the admission to probate ot said instrument as the Last Will and Testament ot Jesse Dorsey lams, deceased, are hereby notified to be present at 10:00 o'clock AM on the 12th day of March. 1973, before the Honorable Olivia C. Gonzales, Probate Judge, at the Taos County Courthouse, Taos, New Mexico, to show cause, it any, why said instrument should not be admitted to probate as the Last Will and Testament of said decedent WITNESS my hand and the seal ot the Probate Court ot Taos County. New Mexico CAROLINE M RODRIGUEZ. Probate Clerk By Diana Romero Deputy (Legal No 1918 PuD Feo 14,21.28 Marcn 7, 1973)
