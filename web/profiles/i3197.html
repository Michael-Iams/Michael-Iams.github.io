    Iams, Taylor Stevens: Newspaper Obituary and Death Notice

    Pittsburgh Tribune-Review (PA) - Wednesday, March 5, 2003
    Deceased Name: Iams, Taylor Stevens
    Taylor Stevens Iams, age 91, of Friendship Village, Upper St. Clair, on Monday, March 3, 2003. Beloved husband of the late Anne Wehr Iams. Devoted father of James Stevens Iams, Esquire, and the late Taylor Edward Iams. Grandfather of Stephen W., David T., Geoffrey B., Keith L., Jonathan C. and Bryan N. Iams. Great-grandfather of Erin Olivia and James P. Iams. Brother of Richard C. Iams and identical twin, the late Charles W. Iams Jr. Taylor was a graduate of the Arnold Academy, now Shadyside Academy, and received his degree in mechanical engineering from Yale University in 1933. He was president of the former Hilltop-West Liberty Lumber Company, founded by his father, Charles W. Iams, and founded the Taylored Industries Inc. of Cheswick, a fabricator and distributor of thermal and electrical insulation materials. Taylor enjoyed the challenge of entrepreneurial endeavors and was strongly committed to community service, serving as a former commissioner of Mt. Lebanon. He was a member of the University Club of Pittsburgh and both he and his beloved Anne were members of Southminster Presbyterian Church, Mt. Lebanon. Friends received in the Mausoleum Chapel of Homewood Cemetery on Friday from 9:30 a.m. until time of service at 10 a.m. Interment will be private. In lieu of flowers, memorials may be made to Odyssey Hospice, Parkway One, Suite 500, Pittsburgh, PA 15275. Arrangements by LAUGHLIN MEMORIAL CHAPEL INC., Mt. Lebanon. www.laughlinfuneralhome.com.
