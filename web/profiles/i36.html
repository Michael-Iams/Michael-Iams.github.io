    ObituaryTranscribed by Laura Nelle Poland Mallett on May 03, 2006. [72]
    Otho Iams, a well known and highly respected resident of Morris township, died at the home of his son, Allen A. Iams, of near Deer Lick, Tuesday, Oct. 18, aged 81 years. He had been in failing health for the past two years. Mr. Iams was a son of Thomas and Delilah Huffman Iams and was born in Morris township, Sept. 4, 1846, where he spent his entire life. He had followed farming and stock raising and was successful. For several years he was a member of the school board of Morris township and at other times had held offices of trust.
    His wife, Sarah Bane Iams, died three years ago. One son, Allan A. Iams, of near Deer Lick, survives; also two sisters, Mrs. Hettie Iams Ray, wife of Hon. J. W. Ray, and Miss Louise Iams, of Waynesburg.
    The funeral services will be held Thursday, at 2 o'clock, conducted by Rev. Paul Brown, assisted by Rev. Franklin Crawford, of Waynesburg. Interment in Green Mount Cemetery.
    "Note" written on my obit is the date Oct. 20, 1927
