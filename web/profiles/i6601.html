    Abner Roy Imes

    Abner Roy Imes, of Flintstone, Md., veteran of World War I, died on Friday, February 20, 1953 at the Veterans Hospital in Altoona. He was born November 19, 1893 in Southampton Township, a son of Ames(sic) and Susan (Bennett) Imes. He was united in marriage with Minnie Bennett, who preceded him in death in 1935. He is survived by one daughter, Mrs. Aveleen Trail of Chaneysville, on brother, Daniel Imes of Cumberland, Md., and the following sisters: Mrs. Lulu Robinette and Mrs. Rebecca Johnson of Cumberland, Mrs. Esta Perdew of Artemas, Mrs. Nora Fletcher of Cumberland, Mrs. Emma Collins of Artemas, Mrs. Amanda Means of Chaneysville, Mrs. Hulda Gross, Mrs. Rose Marshfelt of Cumberland, Mrs. Olive Ewig of Altoona.

    Mr. Imes was a member of the Chaneysville Pentecostal Chursch and was affiliated with Post 8 of the American Legion of Everett. Funeral services were held Sunday afternoon at the Mt. Zion Church at Chaneysville with Pastor Hazel Devore officiating. Interment was in the church cemetery.
