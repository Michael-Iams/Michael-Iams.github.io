Subject: Iams, Annie Laurie Steele obit
Date: Thu, 23 Feb 2006 02:53:10 EST

Annie Laurie Steele Iams, 90, of 99 Christman Ave., Washington, died Monday
Aug. 12, 1985, at 9:45 PM at Canonsburg Hospital.
Born March 23, 1895, in Sturgeon, she was a daughter of Harry C. And Mary C.
Moore Steele.

She was Protestant.

On Sept. 4, 1915, she married William Wylie Iams, who died Dec. 2, 1970.
Surviving are three daughters, Mrs. Virgil “Edna” Miller of Washington,
Mrs. Russell “Ruth Ann” Justin of Aberdeen, S. D., and Mrs. Edward “Martha Jane”
Stewart of Columbiana, Ohio; one son, Paul C. Iams of Pittsburgh; two
sisters, Eva Kuhn of Friendville, Md., and Pauline Steele of Washington; nine
grandchildren, and 12 great-grandchildren.

Deceased are two brothers, one sister, one grandson and one great grandson.

More: Services and interment for Annie Laurie Iams of 99 Christman Ave.,
Washington, who died Monday, Aug. 12, 1985, will be held at the Washington Cemetery at
the discretion of the family. Arrangements are being handled by the Warco
Funeral Home, Washington.
