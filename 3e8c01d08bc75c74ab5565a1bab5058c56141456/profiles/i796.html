    Obituary of John Allen Iiams

    Died –October 28, 1929, John Allen Iiams, at his home in Warsaw.  Mr. Iiams was born June 2, 1869 on the farm known as the Hackler farm in Alexander Township.  He was the oldest child of B.S. and Mary Elizabeth Iiams.  On March 23, 1893, he was married to Elsie Alexander and they had these children:  Gladys, Chicago; Noel, Rockville, Indiana; Smith, Landers, Wyoming; Mrs. Annie Jones and Ernie of Warsaw; and Glen of Kansas City, Kansas; Seth and Ida Belle, deceased.  His wife and these brothers and sisters also survive:  Mrs. Doshia McKenzie and Mrs. Dora Dulaney, Warsaw; Mrs. Nanny Swopes, Quincy and C.W. Iiams of Iiams of Fairfield.
     
    Transcribed from the book:  History of Benton County, Missouri, Vol. 2by  White and Miles. 
    The book was found at the Midwest Genealogy Library in Independence, Mo.
