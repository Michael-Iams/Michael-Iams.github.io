    ObituaryTranscribed by Laura Nelle Poland Mallett on March 16, 2006
    Dennis Iams, Sr., died Jan. 7, 1888, at his home in Morris Township, aged eighty-two years. Deceased had been in poor health for some time. Eight children survive him: Thomas, of Logan County, Ohio, Dennis and Benjamin, of Nineveh, Mrs. John H. Smith, Mrs. Daniel Loughman and Mrs. David Hopkins, of Morris Township. Mrs. Hiran Scott, of Rutan and Mrs, Charles Adamson, of Rogersville. Mr. Iams was a honest, upright man, respected and honored by all who knew him. He had accumulated a large amount of wealth. His remains were interred in the Sycamore burial ground, followed by a large concourse of friends. The funeral sermon was preached by Rev. A. J. Meek.
