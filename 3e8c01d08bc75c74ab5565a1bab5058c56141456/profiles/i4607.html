    EUBANKS, Samantha J. - of Davison, age 82, passed away January 2, 2012 at her home. A Funeral Service will be held 11:00 AM Saturday, January 7, 2012 at Allen Funeral Home, 9136 Davison Rd., Davison; Pastor Jack Dowden officiating. Burial will follow in Flint Memorial Park Cemetery. Visitation will be 5-8 PM Thursday; 12-8 PM Friday and 10-11 AM Saturday. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to Emmanuel Baptist Church.

    Samantha was born in Flint, MI on October 5, 1929, daughter of the late John and Lois (Hawks) Ijames. She married Otis “NR” Eubanks in 1952 and he preceded her in death on January 16, 2006. Samantha was a lifetime member of Emmanuel Baptist Church. She loved cooking, baking and gardening. Her family was everything to her. Samantha was a Nurse at McLaren before retiring in 1993 after many years of service.

    Samantha is survived by her children, Evelyn (Charles) Jackson of Burton, Susan (William) Brown of Scottsdale, AZ, Linda (Richard) Gunter of Davison, Janet Eubanks of Miami, FL, Ned (Cheryl) Eubanks of Millington, Tabitha (Thomas) Johnston of Davison; 15 grandchildren; 3 great-grandchildren; many nieces and nephews. She was preceded in death by 11 brothers and sisters.

    Please share your thoughts with the family at www.allenfuneralhomeinc.com.
