    Obituary
    Edgar “Dennis” Ijames, 84, went home to be with the Lord Wednsday, September 20, 2017. A native of Davie County, he was born July 31, 1933, the son of the late Elias Denton Ijames and Nancy Frances Dennis Ijames. In addition to his parents, he was also preceded in death by a grandson, Benjamin Hedrick, and two sisters, Nancy Myers and Betty George.

    Mr. Ijames is survived by his wife of 52 years, Yvonne Gaither Ijames; two daughters, Suzonne Stratton (Michael) of Winston-Salem and Emily Brown (Shane) of Boonville; one sister, Letty Hinshaw (Paul) of Boonville; and one great grandson, Darren Hedrick.

    A 1952 graduate of Mocksville High School, he later attended Mitchell College. He also served his country in the US Navy. He retired from a 31 year career with Western Electric/AT&T and was a member of New Union United Methodist Church.

    The family will receive friends from 6:00 to 8:00 PM Saturday at Davie Funeral Chapel. The Celebration of Life Service will be conducted at 3:00 PM Sunday at New Union UMC with the pastor, Rev. Eric Lineberry, officiating. Interment will follow in the church cemetery.

    In lieu of flowers, memorials may be considered to New Union UMC, 1869 Sheffield Rd., Mocksville, NC 27028 or to Hospice of Iredell County, 2347 Simonton Rd., Statesville, NC 28625.

    Services are under the direction of Davie Funeral Service of Mocksville. An online guest registry is available at www.daviefuneralservice.com.
