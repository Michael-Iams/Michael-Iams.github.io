    Obituary
    Dorotha J. Goudy
     , 72, a resident of Albuquerque since 1969, died Monday, May 27, 1996. 

    Dorotha was born Jan. 11, 1924, in Grand Ledge, Mich. She retired from civil service after 28 years of employment. 

    She is survived by her sons, Theodore J. Goudy
     and wife, Chu, Donald B. Goudy
     , Lawrence D. Goudy
     and wife, Ruby, and James F. Goudy
     , all of Albuquerque; daughters, Patricia L. Goudy
     , and Roberta Darlene Heckart and husband, Walter, both of Akron; 11 grandchildren, and three great-grandchildren. 

    Services will be held Wednesday, 10:30 a.m., at French Mortuary, University Blvd. Chapel, 1111 University Blvd. N.E., Pastor Richard Encinias officiating. Interment will follow at Sandia Memory Gardens, French Mortuary, 1111 University Blvd. N.E. (French Mortuary, 1-505-843-6333.)
