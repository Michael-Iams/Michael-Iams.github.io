Veronica Constance AcordObituaryApr 15, 1950Hagerstown, Washington, Maryland, United States
VERONA CONSTANCE IMES died suddenly at her home, 818 South Potomac Street yesterday, aged 62 years.

She was a member of Bethel Assembly of God Church.

Surviving, besides husband, are daughters, Pauline Imes, Mrs. Evelyn Smith, Hagerstown; Mrs. Doris Gilbert, Smithsburg; mother, Mrs. Margaret E. Acord, Bartow, W.Va.; sisters, Mrs. edith Collins, Bartow; Mrs. Pearl Burrell, Cresaptown, Md.; Mrs. Sadie Nottingham, Durban, W.Va.; brothers, Olen Acord, Lucian Acord, Akron, Ohio; Edward Acord, Chicago; and two grandchildren.

Funeral services were held at the Norment Funeral Home; interment in Rose Hill Cemetery.
