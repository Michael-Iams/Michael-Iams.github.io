    ObituarySep 13, 1975Salt Lake City, Salt Lake, Utah, United States
    Lloyd Byron Iiams, 68, died at home Sept. 12, 1975 of cancer.
    Born Jan. 31, 1907, Lander, Wyo., to Lloyd E. and Clara Henrietta Minier Iiams. Married Isabel Swanson in 1934, Rock Springs, Wyo.; divorced. Married Naomi Wallin, Sept. 21, 1956, Salt Lake City. Electrical engineer. Member LDS Church, 32nd degree Mason, Kights Templar,. Attended Chicago College of Electrical Engineering. Past president Lander, Wyo. Rotary club. Owned Lander Creamery for 20 years. Fremont County Sheriff in Lander.
    Survivors; wife; daughters, Mrs. Edwin (Shirley) Schmitt, North Caldwell, N.J.; Mrs. Dennis (Janice) Ballatore, West Jordan; stepsons, stepdaughters, Keith R. Wallin, Mrs. Boyd H. (Marjorie) Bronson, Mrs. Robert (Carolyn) Elliot, all Salt Lake City; 18 grandchildren; great-grandchild; Brother, Raymond E., Grand Junction, Colo.
    Burial, Elysian Burial Gardens.

    Salt Lake Tribune (UT) September 13, 1975
