"More MD Deponents" Richard IJAMS was 43 in 1719, 64 in 1740, but 46 in 1740 (b. 1674-76). NEWMAN also says he was 59 in 1729 (b. 1670). William was 44 in 1717 (b. 1673). George was 39 in 1751 but 67 in 1767, so b. 1690/1. "Brothers are William & Richard" [AA 2:345-346]. Since William, called eldest, was born about 1673, then Richard must have been born in 1674+.
NEWMAN's "Anne Arundel Gentry" lists the same six children as above for the first William.
