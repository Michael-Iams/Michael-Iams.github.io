    Note #1

    https://www.findagrave.com/memorial/83900635

    MSA Death Cert #23106

    Mother died when he was 2 weeks old.
    taken to Anne Arundel County and raised by aunt, Elizabeth Drury. (Reynolds, p. 141)

    He enlisted in 23 Aug 1813, as a private in the 51st Maryland Regiment, under Captain Forester (Captain Foster), but was
    discharged a week later on 30 Aug 1813 when the emergency ended. However on 19 Aug 1814, he became a first liutenant in the same regiment, then commanded by Lt. Col Henry Amey, and served until 18 Nov 1814.
    He is credited with serving in the 12 Sep 1814, defense of Baltimore and bombardment of Fort McHenry and was later known as the "Old Defender." He remained in the military after the war and was promoted to Captain.

    Under the Act of 1850, he reserved 40 acres of land in Clayton County, Iowa (assigned to Joshua Barney)
    and by the Act of 1855, he reserved 120 acres of land in Monrow County, WI (assigned to Jonathan R. Warner).
    He applied for a veteran's pension March 7, 1871.
