    ObituaryTranscribed by Laura Nelle Poland Mallett on February 23, 2006. [72]
    Mrs. Elizabeth Iams, of Laboratory, died at her home at 11:15 o’clock Tuesday night, March 28, 1939, after a lingering illness.
    She was born in Amwell Township, May 20, 1858, and was a daughter of Mary Crile. On Jan. 13, 1877, she was married to Z. S. Iams. They lived in Amwell Township until 1909, when they moved to Laboratory.
    Mr. Iams died June 20, 1927.
    Surviving are the following children: I. W. Iams, of Washington, RD 6; Mrs. Minnie B. Edgar, of Pittsburg, and Mrs. May Donahoo, of Laboratory. A daughter, Dale, died in 1905. Also surviving are six grandchildren, and 10 great-grandchildren.
    She was a member of the Lone Pipe Christian Church.
    Funeral services will be held Thursday, March 30, with a short service at the home at 1:30 o’clock and further services in the Lone Pine Christian Church at 2:30 o’clock, in charge of the Rev. John C. Teyssier, of the Laboratory Church, and the Rev. H. Newton Miller, of the Lone Pine Christian Church. Burial will be in the Lone Pine Cemetery.
