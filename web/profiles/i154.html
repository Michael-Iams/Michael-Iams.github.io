Rosetta Walker had a brother David Davis Walker b. 1840 in McLean Co.,
Illinois and d. Oct. 4, 1918 in Kennebunkport, Maine married Dec. 24, 1862 in St.
Louis, Mo. to Martha Adele Beaky. Their son or one of their children was
George Herbert Walker b. Jan. 11, 1875. This George married Lucretia 'Loulie'
Wear and they had at least one daughter Dorothy Walker b. July 1, 1901.
Dorothy married Sheldon Prescott Bush who was born May 15, 1895 in Columbus,
Franklin Co., Ohio [U.S. Senator and started up the Reider Cup in Golf]. they had 5
children: Prescott Sheldon Bush, George Herbert Walker Bush b. June 12,
1924, Nancy Bush, Jonathan James Bush and unknown Bush.
George Herbert Walker Bush married Barbara Pierce and they had at least 6
children:
George W. Bush m. Laura Welch [43 Pres. of U.S.]
John Ellis 'Jeb' Bush Governor of Florida
Robin Bush
Neil Mallon Bush
Marvin Pierce Bush
Dorothy Walker Bush.
