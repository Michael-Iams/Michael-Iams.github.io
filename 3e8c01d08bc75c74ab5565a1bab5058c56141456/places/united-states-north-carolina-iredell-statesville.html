William Clinton IjamesObituaryMar 8, 1976Statesville Record And Landmark, Statesville, Iredell, North Carolina, United States

Monday, March 8, 1976
Statesville Record & Landmark
Page 9-
A Ijames Stricken
HARMONY - William Clinton (Pete) Ijames, 78, of Route 1, Harmony, died Saturday night at his home following a lengthy illness. He was born in Iredell County on Dec. 23,1897, and was a son of the late William and Dollie Gaither Ijames. He was a retired farmer, and was a member of First Baptist Church in Harmony.
Surviving are his wife, Mrs. Sarah Summers Ijames of the home; three sons, Willie Ijames of the home, Bud Ijames of Route 1, Harmony; and Shlrl Ijames of Yadkinville; one foster daughter, Mrs. Odessa Sharpe of Statesville; five brothers, Dan, Sam and James Ijames, all of Statesville, John Ijames of Charlotte, and Henry Ijames of Philadelphia, Pa.
Funeral services for Mr. Ijames will be conducted Tuesday at 2 p.m. at First Baptist Church in Harmony. Rev. Lee Grant will officiate, and burial will follow in the Mt. Nebo Baptist Church cemetery. The family will 'receive friends from 7 to 8 p.m. today at the First Baptist Church. Morrison-Studevent Funeral Home in Mocksville is in charge of arrangements.
