    ObituaryJul 9, 1947Lima News, Lima, Allen, Ohio, United States
    Services for Harvey E. Iiames, 76, who died Wednesday in the hospital of the Allen County Home,will be at 2 p. m. Friday in Lewis Memorial Chapel. Rev. Willard Thomas will officiate and burial will be in Woodlawn Cemetery.

    A resident of Allen County for 60 years, Mr. Iiames resided with a sister, Mrs. Disman of 1136 E. Elm Street. Surviving in addition to Mrs. Disman, are a son Guy of Dayton; two daughters, Mrs. Clyde Custer, 345 S. Jackson Street and Mrs. Paul Kelly, 466 W. McKibben; three brothers James 628 N. West Street, Frank at 340 W. O’Connor Street and William of 853 W. High Street.
