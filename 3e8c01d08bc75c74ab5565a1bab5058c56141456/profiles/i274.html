[from FindaGrave]

On February 4, ANNA MINERVA, in the 64th year of her age, beloved wife of Jacob W. Ijams. [Anne Arundel and Frederick county papers please copy.]
The relatives and friends of the family are respectfully invited to attend the funeral, from No. 1326 North Gilmor street, on Wednesday, February 6, at 2 P.M. Interment private.
-------------------------------------------------
Mrs. Anna Minerva Ijams, 64 years old, died yesterday at her home, 1326 North Gilmor street. She was the wife of Mr. Jacob W. Ijams and the daughter of the late John C. and Minerva Howard. Her grandfather was Col. Noah Phillips, of Frederick, Md.

Mrs. Ijams was born in Ohio, but had lived in this city for many years. She is survived by her husband and four daughters. The daughters are Misses Grace and Florence Ijams, Mrs. Minerva Russell and Mrs. W. B. Brawner, all residents of Baltimore.
