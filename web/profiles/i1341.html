    Note #1

    http://www.findagrave.com/cgi-bin/fg.cgi?page=gr&GRid=19301912

    Abt. 1830 Name appears on 1830 Census Warren County, Ohio. Abt. 1832 Name appears on Tax list for Warren Co., Salem Twp., Ohio. Two Horses valued at $80 and three cattle valued at $24. Bef. 1835 Removed to Shelby County, Ohio . Helped?organizedthe Mt. Gilead Church. Paid taxes on two horses -Value $80; two cattle - value $16.

    In Anna OH Methodist Church (formerly Mount Gilead OH), Thomas, his wife, and children were listed as members in 1835. Thomas bought land in Shelby Co. Dinsmore Tp. OH, known as: S 1/2 of SW 1/4 of Section 17. Thomas was listed among earlysettlers coming from Warren Co. OH, about 1835. Toland is a small family cemetery, now abandoned, in Shelby Co. Dinsmore Tp. OH. Some members of the Iiams family were buried there. It's on the north side of Wells Rd, off of CR 25, one mile westof Anna. It was named after Erasmus B. Toland who at one time owned the land. His wife was Mary Iiams, daughter of Thomas and Mercy (Bonnell) Iiams.
