    WILLIAM IJAMES: Newspaper Obituary and Death Notice
    Greensboro News & Record (NC) - Saturday, April 4, 1992
    Deceased Name: WILLIAM IJAMES
    LEXINGTON - William O. Ijames, 83, of 101 Druid Hill Drive died Friday, April 3, 1992, at Lexington Memorial Hospital.

    Funeral will be at 3:30 p.m. Sunday at Second Reformed United Church of Christ, of which he was a member. Burial will be in Forest Hill Memorial Park.

    A native of Mocksville, he was a retired employee of United Furniture Co. and a member of the Men's Bible Class at his church.

    Surviving are wife, Helen Walker Ijames; son, Ralph L. Walker of Marysville, Calif.; brother, Henry W. Ijames of Lexington; sister, Martha I. Parks of Salisbury; two grandchildren; two great-grandchildren.

    The family will be at Voglers-Piedmont Funeral Home 7-9 p.m. today.

    Memorial contributions may be made to the Dr. A. Odell Leonard Scholarship Fund, 330 N. Church St., Lexington, N.C. 27292.
