    By Roberta Iiams, 19-Aug-2010, Retrieved: 18-Apr-2018

    Samuel Coulson married Charity Dearth. Their daughter Mary Anna Coulson b. 1797 married John Iams b. 1792 and they had seven children, one of which was Charity A. Iams b. 1818 who married Silas Axtell b. 1818. Could be the data in the Atlas needs further investigation.
    ObituaryTranscribed by Laura Nelle Poland Mallett on March 16, 2006. [72]
    Iams
    Died at the home of her daughter, Mrs. C. Axtell, on Franklin Street, this place, Mrs. Annie Iams, aged about 90 years. Mrs. Iams was for many years a member of the Disciple Church and was a consistent Christian. She was the mother of Samuel Iams of Center Township, and grandmother of Dr. J. T. Iams, of this place. Her remains were taken to Burnsville, Washington, Co., for interment.
