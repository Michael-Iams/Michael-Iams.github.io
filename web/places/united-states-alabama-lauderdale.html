The 1866 Lauderdale County state census lists Joseph Ijams 4 houses down from Eliaa Thrasher. (It is not certain whether this was "that man Thrasher" or not). Joseph owned several tracts of
peace in 1842'and in 1871 ran for County treasurer. In June 1880, he took the oath of office for the Probate Court.
Joseph's wife Elizabeth passed away at her home on July 16, 1867. Her obituary states that she had been a member of the Christian Church for 30 years. Joseph passed away in July or August 1882, and was buried in Simmons Cemetery next to Elizabeth. As he died, intestate, his estate was probated in August, 1883.
