    ADMINISTRATION
    We, A.B. Hamm, J.C. Ijams, J.R. Hurly and J.M. Hamm are bound to the state of Tennesee in the penalty of $3,000.00.
    Witness our hands this 6th day of Decr. 1875.
    The condition of this obligation is such, That, whereas, the above bound A.B. Hamm has been appointed administrator of B.C. Ijams, deceased.

    Signed: A.B. Hamm, J.C. Ijams, J.R. Hurley, J.M. Hamm Acknowledged and approved Dec. 6th, 1875 C. Shull, County Court Clerk
