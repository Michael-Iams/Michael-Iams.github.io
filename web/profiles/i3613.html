    March 17, 1949 (died March 6, 1949)

    Obituary
    Ira Russell Iiams, son of John and Mary Kraft Iiams, was born December 15, 1861, in Champaign County near Urbana, Illinois and departed this life Sunday, March 6, 1969, having reached the age of 87 years, 3 months and 23 days.

    About 1875 with his parents he moved to oblong vicinity where he remained until the time of his death.

    He was united in marriage to Hulda Jane Kieffer on September 13, 1883. To this union were born three daughters, Elsie Edith, Effie Mae Smith, and Mary Margurite Kinter. His wife and two daughters preceded him in death.

    As a young man he was converted and united with the Seeds Chapel Methodist Church. While being quiet and undemonstrative, he was a firm believes in God and a good husband and father.

    Although he suffered from rheumatism for a number of years and found it difficult to walk, he did daily chores until August, 1946, when he became bedfast.

    Mr. Iiams is survived by one daughter, Marguerite Kintner, a son-in-law, Lester L. Kintner, and one grandson, Tommie Kintner, of Russell, Kansas, one sister, Hettie Jackson of Marion, Indiana, two half sisters, Sarah Griffy of Robinson, Ilinois, and Ida Fowler of Oblong and one half brother, Otis Iiams of Oblong, also many nieces and nephews.
