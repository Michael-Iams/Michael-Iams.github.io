ObituaryTranscribed by Laura Nelle Poland Mallett on March 06, 2006 [72]
Hazel H. Collins Iams, 92, of the Thomas Campbell Apartments, died at 4:15 AM Tuesday, Feb. 5, 1985, in Allegheny General Hospital, Pittsburgh, following a short illness.
She was born June 14, 1892, in Washington, daughter of Hugh and Elizabeth Schwear Collins.
Mrs. Iams was a member of the First Christian Church, CWF, Golden Age Club, Loyal Women's Class, Claudia Circle and Lucy Mounts Class.
On Sept. 15, 1914, she married Bert H. Iams, who died Sept. 15, 1952.
Surviving are one granddaughter, Mrs. David "Joyce Ann" Everly of Augusta, Georgia; one sister, Mary Reillo of Pittsburgh; several nieces and nephews; one great-grandson, Christian Mark Everly of Augusta.
One son, Albert N. Iams, and three sisters, Mrs. Hallie Greco, Mrs. Nannie McKinney and Mrs. Nellie Ellis, are deceased.
More:
Friends of Hazel H. Iams of the Thomas Campbell Apartments, who died Tuesday, Feb. 5, 1985, will be received from 2-9 PM Thursday in the Warco Funeral Home Inc., S. Timothy Warco, Supervisor, Wilson at East Katherine Avenues, Washington, where services will be held at 11 AM Friday, Feb. 8, with the Dr. Robert E. Pebley, pastor of the First Christian Church, Washington, officiating. Interment in Washington Cemetery.
