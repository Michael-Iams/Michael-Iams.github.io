    In the fall of 1865, Harrison (Johnston) paid John and Rebecca Iams a total of $2,600 for a tract of 84 acres near Wind Ridge in Rich Hill Township, Greene County. The family is enumerated there in the 1870 census, although Greene County tax assessment records show that ownership of this acreage was transferred in 1870 to W.R. Sickles.
