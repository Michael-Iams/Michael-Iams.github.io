ObituaryJul 11, 1887Howard, Maryland, United States
On July 9, 1887, CECILIA S. IJAMS, in the 74th year of her age, widow of the late J. P. Ijams. Her funeral will take from her late residence, in Howard county, this (Monday) afternoon, July 11, at three o'clock. Carriages will meet the 1:30 P.M. train from Camden Station at Ilchester.
-----------------------------------------------------
Mrs. Cecilia S. Ijams, widow of the late ex-Sheriff J. Plummer Ijams, of the first district of Howard county, died suddenly at her late home, near Ilchester, about nine o'clock yesterday morning, aged 74 years. Mrs. Ijams leaves several married children and a number of grandchildren.
