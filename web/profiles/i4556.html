    James Robert Ijames
    Born in Owensboro, KY on Apr. 20, 1933
    Departed on Jan. 19, 2009 and resided in Auburn, KY.
    Service: Friday, Jan. 23, 2009
    12 pm
    Cemetery: Kentucky Veterans Cemetery Central
    James Robert "Bob" Ijames, 75, of Auburn, Kentucky died Monday January 19, 2009, at Logan Memorial Hospital
    in Russellville, KY. He was born on April 20, 1933 in Owensboro to the late Jasper Newton Ijames, II and Ruby
    Crabtree Ijames. He was of the Baptist faith and was an Air Force veteran.
    He is survived by his five children, Lisa Ijames, Barbara Ijames and Richard Ijames all of California and Kenneth
    Ijames and David Ijames both of Tennessee; a sister, Shelia Ijames of Owensboro and four brothers, Charles
    Ijames, Richard Ijames and Jasper Newton Ijames, III all of Owensboro and Lee Tyler Ijames of Louisville, KY.
    Graveside services are at 12:00 p.m.(ET) on Friday, January 23, 2009, at Kentucky Veterans Cemetery Central in
    Radcliff, Kentucky. There will be no visitation. James H. Davis Funeral Home in Owensboro is in charge of
    arrangements.
